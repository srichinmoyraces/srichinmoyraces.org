Mac Masters Meet 1994

On June 4, 1994 Sri Chinmoy competed in the Mac Masters Games at St. Johns University.

Sri Chinmoy's electronic time was 15.14 at a weight of 142 pounds. He took 64.75 strides.
Sri Chinmoy came in 4th in his age category and 5th overall. The other runner pictured—Tom—ran 14.98.

Sri Chinmoy Masters Games New York 1994


On July 16, 1994 Sri Chinmoy competed in the Sri Chinmoy Masters Games in New York.


His first time was 15.80 at a weight of 147 pounds. Sri Chinmoy took 72 strides.



In his second race he ran 15.07 and took 69 strides.


Sri Chinmoy injured himself in the second race and could not train or compete for 11 months.
