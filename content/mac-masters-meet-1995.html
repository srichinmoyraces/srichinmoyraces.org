Mac Masters Meet 1995


On June 10, 1995 Sri Chinmoy competed in the Mac Masters Games in New York at St Johns University.

His electronic time was 16.62 at a weight of 160 pounds. Sri Chinmoy took 73 strides. This was his first sprint after his previous injury. Sri Chinmoy had only trained for 2 days prior to the race.
