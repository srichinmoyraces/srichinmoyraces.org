Self-Transcendence 2-mile Race, San Diego, CA

By Rupantar LaRusso author bio »
Sunday, 1 November, 2009

About the author:

Rupantar has been the race director of the Sri Chinmoy Marathon Team since 1985, having been asked by Sri Chinmoy to serve in that capacity. As well as working on the big races the US Marathon Team organise each year - the 3100 Mile Race and the Six and 10 Day Race - he also spends a considerable amount of time archiving the Marathon Team's 40 year history on this website.

FREE 2-Mile Race

As part of the global series of 2-mile races, the San Diego event offers a measured course, timing, and refreshments to participants of all ages and paces.

Year-round: Join us each Saturday at 8:00 a.m. in Trolley Barn Park (Adams Ave. and Florida St.).

Summer Series: Thursday evenings in Balboa Park. Stay tuned for 2012 dates and times.

All races are FREE of charge. No pre-registration is necessary. Just show up and join the fun!

Sponsored by Sri Chinmoy Marathon Team, Jyoti-Bihanga Restaurant, and Pilgrimage of the Heart Yoga. For more information, please call 619-280-0735.

National 100 Mile Cycle Race

By Vilas Silverton
18 July, 2010

Tejvan Pettinger of Sri Chinmoy Cycling Team, finished 15th in the UK National 100 mile time trial championship. On a windy day, he finished the 100 mile race in a time of 3.52.18 (25.8mph). It was a new club record, despite a slow second half due to heat.

This weekend, Tejvan finished.

  • 2nd in North Hampshire 10 mile TT (21.00)
  • 1st - London West Cycling Association 50 Mile TT (1.53.57)
